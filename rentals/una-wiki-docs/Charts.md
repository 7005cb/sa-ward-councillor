The app consists of 4 charts, after installation are displayed on the Dashboard (/page/dashboard).

After changing any of the settings, the diagrams will be rebuilt within a few minutes.

### Top contents by likes

Displays the most popular N content items (defined in the _Max number of items in the chart_) from the apps selected in the _Enabled for modules_ for the period defined in _Interval in days_.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/wiki/unaio/una/images/charts/001.png)

**Settings**

1. Enabled for modules - select apps whose content is taken when collecting data
2. Max number of items in the chart - number of content items, display on the chart
3. Interval in days (0 for all data) - The time interval to collect data for chart

### Most active profiles

Displays the most popular N profiles (defined in the M_ax number of items in the chart_) from the apps selected in Profiles modules and active in the apps selected in _Modules to count content_ in the period defined in _Interval in days_.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/wiki/unaio/una/images/charts/002.png)

**Settings**

Profiles modules - list of profile apps for collect data

1. Modules to count content in - list of content apps  for collect data
2. Max number of items in the chart - number of content items, display on the chart
3. Interval in days (0 for all data) - The time interval to collect data for chart

### Most followed profiles

Displays the most popular N profiles (defined in the _Max number of items in the chart_) from the modules selected in _Most followed profiles_ for the period defined in _Interval in days_.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/wiki/unaio/una/images/charts/003.png)

**Settings**

1. Most followed profiles - list of profile apps for collect data
2. Max number of items in the chart - number of content items, display on the chart
3. Interval in days (0 for all data) - The time interval to collect data for chart

### Content growth

Displays the growth of content for the list of modules defined in Enabled for the period defined in _Interval in days_ and grouped by _Group by._

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/wiki/unaio/una/images/charts/004.png)

**Settings **

1. Enabled for  - list of apps for collect data
2. Interval in days (0 for all data) - The time interval to collect data for chart
3. Group by - interval for groupping data (day/week/year)
