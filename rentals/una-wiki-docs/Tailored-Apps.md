Tailored Apps are created specifically for customers under Enterprise Plan. They may be based on some of the Core Apps or can be completely unique. Tailored Apps are created to client specification and generally include some degree of the customer's intellectual property. Therefore, Tailored Apps do not appear in Market and can not be re-distributed without the customer permission. 

Tailored Apps may be hosted on the private Sandbox accessible by UNA Team and the customer only, for development and testing purposes. Code of the Tailored apps may also be hosted in a private repository, created for each individual project. 

All Tailored apps must be installed and updated manually by the customer or UNA Team.
